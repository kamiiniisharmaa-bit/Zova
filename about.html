About Zova

rooted in style, built for tomorrow San Francisco, CA est. 2023

Our Story

Zova was born from a simple idea: fashion should be effortless, sustainable, and accessible. What started as a small pop-up in San Francisco has grown into a global community of conscious creators and style-seekers. We believe that what you wear should not only look good but also feel good — for you and for the planet.

Every piece in our collection is designed with purpose: timeless silhouettes, premium fabrics, and ethical craftsmanship. We work directly with artisans and manufacturers who share our commitment to fair labor and environmental responsibility.

Our north star: "To inspire a world where fashion meets function, and every purchase plants a seed for a better future."

Our Mission

We're on a mission to redefine modern essentials. We aim to:

  • Reduce waste: By using recycled materials and minimizing packaging.
  • Empower communities: Partnering with ethical manufacturers and giving back to local causes.
  • Inspire confidence: Creating versatile, durable pieces that make you feel unstoppable.

Sustainable

Ethical

Inclusive

Quality

What We Stand For

At Zova, we believe that small choices create big change. That's why we prioritize:

  • Slow fashion: We release curated drops, not endless collections. Every piece is made to be worn and loved for years.
  • Radical transparency: We share our supply chain, our impact, and our progress — the good and the challenging.
  • Community first: Our customers are at the heart of everything we do. Your feedback shapes our designs and our direction.

Community & Impact

Our Impact

Since our founding, we've planted over 5,000 trees, diverted 12 tons of textile waste from landfills, and donated 3% of our profits to organizations supporting education and environmental justice.

We're just getting started. Every Zova purchase contributes to a better world — and we're grateful to have you on this journey with us.
